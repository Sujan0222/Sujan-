World Cup 2022 Qatar

The 2022 FIFA World Cup is an international football tournament set to take place in Qatar from November 21 to December 18, 2022. It was the first time that the World Cup will be held in the Middle East, and the first time that it take place in November and December, rather than June and July. A total of 32 teams will participate in the tournament, which was played in eight stadiums across five host cities. The final was held at the Lusail Iconic Stadium, which is set to have a capacity of 80,000 spectators.
